A lot of beautiful places nearby.

One of the extreme sources of Natural Beauty, a place that offers unlimited fun experience for the people all around the globe,the green carpet that welcomes you with its pleasing climate. We have listed some of the places here, but there are a many more beautiful places in Wayanad.

Karappuzha Dam

The exuberant water, drenched hills, spectacular environment, and green islands make it a perfect spot for tourists from all around. Placed on the both sides of Karapuzha River, the dam is one of the largest earth dams of India. The best part of everything is that Caffeine Villa is just 12 km away from this eternal beauty.

Kuruva Island

Spread over 950 acres, the geographical specifications of this island makes this place evergreen with a peacefully ambience. The famous boat rides on bamboo rafts is one of the most exciting things about Kuruva Island for tourists. 13.3 km away from Caffeine Villa you could dwell deep into this packets of island.

Bandipur

Bandipur National Park is regarded as one of the most beautiful and the better-managed national parks of India. Located amidst the picturesque surroundings of the towering Western Ghat Mountains having the second most tiger population in India. 30kms from Caffeine Villa, but worth the distance.

Pookot Lake

Do you wish to pedal a boat all around India? Make your wish come true at Pookode Lake just 19.3 km away from Caffeine Villa. The wonderful freshwater lake has the shape of Indian map. Nestled between evergreen forest and Western Ghats, the lake is spread over 13 acre and is 40 m deep. Boating in this lake is a unique experience.

Meenmutty Falls

At a distance of 18.6 km from Caffeine Villa you would reach the the largest and most spectacular waterfall in Wayanad. Gushing down through various layers, Meenmutty Falls downpour from a height of over 800 feet, it is a spectacular fall situated inside thick forest flowing through rocky platform.

Edakkal Caves

Edakkal Caves is just 16.6 km away from Caffeine Villa. It is considered to be one of the earliest centres of human habitation. Witness the extreme beauty of rock carvings within the two natural rock formations believed to have been formed by a large split in a huge rock. Trek up to the historical cave for an everlasting experience.

Ancient Jain Temple

At a distance of 16.6 km from Caffeine Villa, you reach one of the most important and ancient Jain temples in Kerala. The Ancient Jain Temple at Sulthan Bathery dates back to the 13th century. The temple is said to have been built in the prevalent architectural style of the then reigning Vijayanagar dynasty.

Banasura Sagar Dam

Banasura Sagar Dam holds the record of being the largest earth dam in India and the second largest in Asia. The Dam is placed in the foothills of Banasura hills,which is the third largest peak in the Western Ghats. A boating trip in the lake and the dam is also a unique experience for the travellers. Just 16.4 km away from Caffeine Villa.

Heritage Museum

At a distance of 14.8 km from Caffeine Villa, you could explore the rare collection of several ancient instruments and many tribal artifacts of 14th - 16th century sculptures at The Wayanad Heritage Museum. The museum exhibits fabulous items from tribal communities which dates back to the second century AD.
